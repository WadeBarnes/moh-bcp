This form is to be completed by applicants for facilities that do not have a valid Medical Services Plan Facility Number.

All information on this form must be authorized by the responsible Practitioner for the facility (the Administrator) to which the MSP Facility Number may be issued. If there are any changes to the information contained in this document after it has been submitted, the Administrator must submit an Application to Cancel or Change Details for Facilities with an MSP Facility Number form.

To complete this application form you will require the following information:

  • Facility Administrator information including name and Medical Services Plan practitioner number
  • Facility information including facility name, address and postal code

In addition to applying for a Medical Services Plan Facility Number you will be able to request that the Business Cost Premium be applied to the facility.
