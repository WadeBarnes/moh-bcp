Who can complete this form?

This form is to be completed by an applicant to cancel an MSP facility number OR change details of an Medical Services Plan (MSP) Facility Number.

What can the form be used for?

This form may be used to:

  • Change the address of the facility associated with an MSP Facility Number, provide the effective date of the address change, the previous address and the new address
  • Change details about the facilities Business Cost Premium (BCP) enrolment
  • Change the facilities responsible administrator

What do you need to fill out this form?

To complete this form you will require at least the following information:

  • The name of the current Facility Administrator
  • The Facility (practice, clinic, site) name and MSP Facility Number
  • Current and/or updated information you wish to change
